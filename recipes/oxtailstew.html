Oxtail Stew

Oxtail Stew , A Taste Of Jamaica

This delicious dish though not for the calorie counter is filling and tasty.It is a staple in any Jamaican household.

It may be very pricey in and out of the island though.Ensure your research if purchasing in a restaurant setting.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound oxtail per person
  • Jamaican seasoning
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot with oil till smoking
  2. Season Oxtail with jamaican seasoning
  3. Add black pepper , salt , scotch bonnet to taste
  4. Add water , thicken till suitable
