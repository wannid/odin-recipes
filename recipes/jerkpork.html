Jerk Pork

Dry Spicy Barbecue Pork , A Jamaican Delicacy

This delightful dish is a Jamaican original.It is made locally with pork belly smoked to perfection over pimento wood and leaves.

Boston in the Portland parish of Jamaica is home to the most popular and arguably the best Jerk in the world.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Jamaican Seasoning
  • Fresh Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Season pork belly with jamaican seasoning.
  2. Add scotch bonnet , salt and black pepper to taste.
  3. Let pork cure in the fridge for at least 1 day.
  4. Smoke slowly outdoors either over coal covered with pimento leaves or alternately in the oven.
  5. Remove when meat is cooked through.
