Curried Goat

Jamaican Curried Goat Where India Meets Jamaica

This local delicacy has is only identical to the indian dish in name.The difference lies in the powder used to season it.

It is thought that immigrants from the east made their own version of this favourite dish and now it is considered to be its own wonder.

Ingredients

  • Goat or mutton belly.
  • Irish Potatoes
  • Jamaican Curried Powder
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper

Steps

  1. Rub curried powder generously over mutton.
  2. Leave 24 hours to cure in fridge.
  3. Heat a large pot , add 2 teaspoons of oil , heat till smoking.
  4. Add mutton and potatoes.
  5. Stir pot till simmering , add salt , black pepper to taste , serve when gravy thickens.
